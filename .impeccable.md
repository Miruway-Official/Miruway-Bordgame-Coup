# Impeccable Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Friends playing together — casual social gaming on mobile, passing a phone or playing online. Fun and clarity are the priority. Players may include new players who need quick orientation.

### Brand Personality
**Dramatic · Sharp · Cinematic**
A premium card game with Thai-language UI. The experience should feel like a secret society gathering — tense, sophisticated, memorable. Not a children's game, not a hardcore simulacrum — a stylish social deduction game.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Dark Editorial × Card Game Drama**
- Inspired by: Hearthstone's card depth + editorial magazine layouts
- Primary feeling: High-contrast, type-driven, cards are the visual heroes
- Background: Deep navy-slate (#080e16), NOT flat black
- Typography: Cormorant Garamond (display/dramatic moments) + DM Sans (UI/body) — replacing Cinzel/Inter
- Gold: Warm amber-gold (oklch 72% 0.12 80), NOT neon yellow
- Character cards: Large, colorful, with strong color bands — the most visually rich element
- Action panel: Full-width bottom tray with strong typographic hierarchy
- NO glassmorphism, NO glow borders as decoration, NO generic dark-mode purple gradients

### Design Principles
1. **Cards are heroes** — character cards should be the largest, most visually rich element on screen
2. **Type creates hierarchy** — size and weight contrasts do the work, not decorative borders
3. **Drama through restraint** — generous whitespace makes tense moments hit harder
4. **Every coin counts** — coins shown as visual dots/gems, not just plain numbers
5. **Mobile-first, thumb-first** — all primary actions within thumb reach at the bottom
